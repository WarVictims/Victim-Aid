Why This Matters

War and conflict have separated families across borders. Our mission is to reunite loved ones using verified information, trained search teams, and global coordination. With your help, we can act fast and save lives.

Rescue Request Form

How It Works

1. Submit Information

Fill out the rescue request form with details about your missing loved one — name, last known location, and a recent photo. Every detail increases the chance of success.

2. Our Team Verifies

Our trained volunteers and partner organizations verify the details to rule out misinformation and prepare an action plan. We’ll contact you if more information is needed.

3. Search & Coordination

We collaborate with local authorities, humanitarian groups, and field teams to initiate the search — whether it’s refugee camps, hospitals, or checkpoints.

4. Receive Updates

We provide you with regular updates and next steps. If the person is found, we help you reconnect through secure communication and transport arrangements where possible.
