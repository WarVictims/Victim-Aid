Our story begins with purpose

Helping War Victims was founded in 2022 in Lithuania, born from the urgent and growing needs of civilians affected by the devastating consequences of war. What began as a response to destruction quickly evolved into a committed mission to restore human dignity, provide life-saving assistance, and build pathways to recovery for those left in the ruins.

At the heart of our work is a belief that no one should be forgotten. We focus on real people and their stories — children, families, veterans — individuals who carry both visible and invisible scars. Every action we take is grounded in compassion, and every program is designed not only to meet immediate needs but also to empower long-term renewal.

From rebuilding homes and offering trauma support to giving orphans access to education and technology, we create hope where it has been lost. Our journey is defined by empathy, accountability, and an unwavering dedication to protect life and opportunity, even in the darkest places.

Our vision is bold but clear: to be a bridge between despair and renewal — and to ensure that, together, we create a future where survival transforms into strength.
