Frequently Asked Questions

Anyone who has a missing loved one due to war or displacement can submit a request through our Rescue page.

We currently work in regions affected by conflict, including Ukraine, Israel, Iran, Syria, and parts of Africa.

Due to the complexity and urgency of many rescue missions, a service fee may apply. We work with local teams, transport services, and agencies that require funding. However, we always do our best to keep costs minimal and transparent.

While no rescue is guaranteed, we use trusted networks, local field partners, and humanitarian liaisons to increase the chances of success. Many families have been reunited through our dedicated search process.

You’ll need your full name, contact details, your loved one’s name, last known location, a recent photo (if available), and any specific circumstances. The more detailed, the better.

It depends on location, accessibility, and urgency. Some rescues happen within days, others may take weeks. We keep you informed every step of the way.

Yes. Donations help cover the cost of transport, search teams, medical aid, and communication services. Every contribution brings hope to another family waiting for help.

In urgent or life-threatening cases, we may be able to connect you with donors or sponsor assistance. Please still submit the request — no one is turned away without review.
